# 💳 Grafty Credits: The Vendor's Guide to Messaging

Welcome to Grafty! Our mission is to provide you with the most powerful and transparent WhatsApp automation tools. This guide explains how our credit system works and how it saves you money as your business grows.

## 🌟 Why do I need Credits?
To run high-performance automations—like **Interactive Flows**, **CRM Syncing**, **Drip Campaigns**, and **Broadcasts**—Grafty uses a prioritized message engine. Your credit balance powers this engine.

---

## 🛤️ Choose Your Path: Managed vs. Direct

We offer two ways to host your WhatsApp account. You can switch between them at any time from your settings.

### 1. Managed Mode (Default)
**Ideal for**: Businesses that want zero hassle and one single invoice.
- **How it works**: You pay Grafty for everything. We handle the Meta billing in the background.
- **Cost**: Meta's standard conversation rates + a 15% platform markup.
- **Billing**: 100% deducted from your Grafty Wallet.

### 2. Direct Mode (Recommended for High Volume)
**Ideal for**: Tier 2 companies and growing startups looking to save on costs.
- **How it works**: You connect your own Meta App and pay Meta directly for their conversation fees using your own credit card.
- **Cost**: **Zero Markup**. You pay Meta's base price + a flat **₹0.05 Orchestration Fee** to Grafty per message.
- **Billing**: Meta costs go to your credit card; the ₹0.05 fee is deducted from your Grafty Wallet.

---

## ❓ FAQ: Why do I need credits in "Direct Mode"?
Even if you pay Meta directly, Grafty provides the "brain" for your WhatsApp. Your credits cover:
1.  **Flow Orchestration**: Running complex logic, buttons, and API nodes in your chats.
2.  **Infrastructure**: Priority message queuing and high-speed delivery (TPS).
3.  **Real-time Analytics**: Tracking your campaign performance and ROI.
4.  **CRM Integration**: Automatically syncing leads and data across your business tools.

**Benefit**: Direct Mode usually saves you **15-20% on total costs** compared to other platforms, even after our ₹0.05 fee!

---

## ⚡ How to Recharge
1. Navigate to **Dashboard > Settings > Billing**.
2. Click **Add Credits**.
3. Choose your amount and complete the payment via **Razorpay** (supports UPI, Cards, Netbanking).
4. Your balance updates instantly.

---

## 🛡️ Fair Usage & Balance
- **Low Balance Alerts**: We’ll notify you when your balance drops below ₹100.
- **Automatic Pause**: If your balance hits zero, your automated flows and broadcasts will pause to prevent message loss.
- **Validity**: Your purchased credits never expire.
